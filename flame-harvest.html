🔥 Flame Harvest

A cozy idle clicker game where you tap flames and gather magical fruit-based resources!

🧱 Features:

  • 🔥 Click the flame to earn Flamecoins.
  • 🍓 Collect Coal, Tiny Flames, Spark Dust, and Ember Cores.
  • 🏡 Build the Firetree House as your main hub.
  • 💎 Upgrade with Frost Stars and unlock automation tools.
  • ⚒️ Helpers, upgrades, city view and more!

🎮 Status:

Currently in early development. Launching soon for mobile and web!
